Cloud-Native Infrastructure at NSF NCAR

CIRRUS is NSF NCAR's cloud-native platform leveraging Kubernetes clusters across two sites. Built on GitOps principles with Argo CD, CIRRUS provides scalable, production-ready infrastructure for deploying and managing applications.
